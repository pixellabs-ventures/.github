## Live now

**ThreadCast** &nbsp;·&nbsp; Pixel Apps
Reddit threads, turned into podcast-like audio: a distinct voice per commenter,
four on-device neural TTS engines, a sleep mode, and zero tracking.
[Site](https://threadcast.app) ·
[Chrome](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/threadcast/dhbcjkjfhebbjffojpcmfoffhchijpkn) ·

**Kahraba Fa** &nbsp;·&nbsp; Pixel Partners
A Moroccan residential-solar platform we built and co-own: a 2-minute personalised
solar study, a WhatsApp sales bot, and a full installer back-office, in French and Darija.
[Site](https://kahrabafa.pixellabs.ventures/fr)

More in progress across every branch. The roadmap is part of the story.
